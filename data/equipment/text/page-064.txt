                                                          MOBILE COMM
                                                          A mobile comm unit is a portable telecommunications
                                                          device/computer/camera, ranging in size from a bulky
                                                          handset to a slim watch or pen-like cylinder – in today’s
                                                          world we call them mobile phones or cell phones.
                                                          Larger comms have physical controls and screens,
                                                          while smaller units either project data and control
                                                          displays onto nearby surfaces, have fold-out plastic
                                                          screens or connect to cybernetics. Comms have only
                                                          short-range transmission and reception capabilities
                                                          – typically five kilometres – but most technologically
                                                          advanced worlds have planetwide comm networks,
