                                                                                          PROTOTECH
                                                                                          A prototype device uses lower tech, substitute or
                                                                                          other prototype components to build an item normally
                                                                                          constructed at up to two Tech Levels greater technology.
                                                                                          These devices are non-standard, larger, more expensive
                                                                                          and often less reliable than their standard counterparts.
