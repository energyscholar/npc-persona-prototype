## Payment/Credits Summary

### Scout Service Offer
- **Upfront:** Cr1000 per traveller (incidental expenses)
- **During Mission:** Life support, fuel costs covered
- **Completion:** Hotel + Cr1000 expenses during week-long ship checkout
- **Reward:** One-year lease on Highndry (detached duty terms)
- **Bonus:** Small payment for first-hand starport reports

### Greener's Offer
- **Amount:** Cr3000 flat fee
- **Form:** Case full of Imperial Credits
- **Negotiable:** Can be talked up slightly with respectful approach
- **Hard Limit:** Won't go higher - already paid previous crew who fled

### Local Costs
- Weapon storage at starport: Cr10 per week
- Hostel accommodation: Very cheap, barracks-style

| Crater descent | Athletics | Average (8+) | Final climb down |

### Altitude Sickness Effects
- **Mild (1 failed check):** DM-2 on all checks
- **Moderate (2 failed checks):** DM-4 on all checks
- **Severe (3 failed checks):** DM-6 on all checks

### Recovery
- Natural: -1 penalty per day resting at same/lower altitude
- With oxygen: Dispels symptoms 1-2 hours (then returns)
- Controlled environment (8 hours): -2 penalty per day

### Ship Repair Checks

| System | Time | Skill |
|--------|------|-------|
| Flight controls/nav (bridge) | 1D+2 man-hours | Electronics |
| General electronics (dispersed) | 1D+2 man-hours | Electronics |
| Power systems (engineering) | 1D+2 man-hours | Electronics |
| Drive systems (engineering) | 1D+2 man-hours | Electronics |
| Computer purge/upload | Varies | Electronics (computer) |

**Note:** Most work can be done by unskilled person with guidance (remove-and-replace procedure). Difficulty not specified - referee determines.

**Modifier:** DM-2 to all Pilot/Engineer checks if pre-flight tests skipped.

### Eruption Flight Checks

| Check | Difficulty | Notes |
|-------|------------|-------|
| All Pilot checks in eruption zone | Difficult (10+) | Smoke, ash, rock "missiles" |
| Terrain mapping | Electronics (sensors) | Couple of overflights |
| Flow rate measurement | Electronics (sensors) or Recon | Half hour+ to emplace sensors |

### Save the Ship Sequence

**Initial Impact:** 3D damage base
- -1D if headed into cloud
- -1D if strapped in
- Very Difficult (12+) DEX to cling for -1D

**Required Checks:**
1. Control reboot: Average (8+) Electronics (computer) OR Difficult (10+) Pilot
2. Power override: Average (8+) Engineer (power) - must leave seat
3. Pilot control: Average (8+) Pilot - needs both above completed first

**Pilot Check Results:**
- 3 successes: Full recovery, climb to safety
- 2 successes: Hit water, skip, recover. 2D/1D/0 damage
- 1 success: Dive into bay, embed in seabed 12m down. 3D/2D/1D damage
- 0 successes: Belly-flop. 6D/4D/2D damage. Ship sinks.

## Location Details

### 567-908 Waypoint
- Officially uninhabited (census counts permanent residents only)
- ~24 people on-planet (half scouts, half civilian contractors)
- Browny-grey rock everywhere - colors so uniform it's hard to judge distances
- Starport: Cleared bedrock landing area (up to 600 tons)
- Lake 1km southwest - water source for fuel and drinking
- Refuelling: Lengthy (water cracking for hydrogen)
- Observatory on ridge 3-4km north
- 3 ATVs at port (not available for sightseeing)
- "Mandel's Prang" - sculpture made from crashed ATV

### Walston Startown
- Village-sized (walk around in minutes)
- Buildings mostly underground with top floor at surface
- Connected in complexes with common entrances and garages
- Indoor dress: Kilts, light shirt/tunic, sandals/bare feet
- Vargr wear bright colors; humans wear sober (grey, white, black, blue, dark red)

**Facilities:**
- Couple decent hotels
- Modest hostel (barracks accommodation, basic meals, very cheap)
- Couple restaurants
- Handful of shops (local and offworld goods)
- Port Authority office (ship records - free access, in person only)

### Mount Salbarii
- Active volcano (not extinct as believed)
- Crater contains lake with island
- Highndry parked on crater island
- 1,400m elevation (from sea level)
- Lava tubes run through mountain
- Deep cavern formed by ancient lava bubble connects tubes

### The Highndry (in crater)
- All hatches closed
- Everything inside dumped outside (crew stripped valuables)
- Mess made worse by Tensher's wolf searching for food
- Access panels open, covers off, components strewn
- Ration packets ripped open (by animal)
- Kimbley's food bowl among debris
- Power plant down, batteries nearly drained
- Enough power for airlock entry code (manual door opening)
- Seismic charges aboard (scattered but intact)
- Survey equipment intact (crew didn't tamper)
- Life rafts (x2, capacity 6 each) in survival kits
